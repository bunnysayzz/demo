IMPORTANT: Before deploying to Heroku

1. After creating your Heroku app, update the URL in appcast.xml:
   - Open server/updates/appcast.xml
   - Replace "http://localhost:8000" with "https://your-app-name.herokuapp.com"
   - Example: <enclosure url="https://your-app-name.herokuapp.com/AppleAI-1.1.2.zip" ... />

2. Deploy to Heroku using the instructions in README.md

3. Test your update server by visiting:
   - https://your-app-name.herokuapp.com/
   - https://your-app-name.herokuapp.com/appcast.xml

4. Update your app to use the new update URL by modifying the URL in your app's update manager.
